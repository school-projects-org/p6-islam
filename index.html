P7 3.2 Aiding People through Science

Hayat Sindi is widely recognized as one of the most influential Muslim women in the world. She is a scientist who has made several groundbreaking contributions in the fields of medicine. One of her most widely known contributions is a tiny piece of paper- that's saved millions of lives. Check out /subpages/device/index.html for more.



One of the fundamental beliefs of Islam is, in layman's terms, to be kind and helpful. Sindi does just that, helping make medical diagnoses and treatments cheaper, and quicker. She's not in it for the money, but legitimately wanting to help the world. Islamic culture believes strongly in acts of kindness to others, being benefactoring, etc.
